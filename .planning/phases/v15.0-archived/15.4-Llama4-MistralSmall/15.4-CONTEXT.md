---

## Implementation Notes

### Llama 4 Architecture
- Mixture of Experts (MoE) with sparse activation
- Scout: 16 experts, 2 active
- Maverick: 128 experts, 8 active
- GQA for key-value heads

### Mistral Small Architecture
- MoE with expert routing (8 experts, 2 active)
- Similar to Mistral-7B-v0.3 architecture
- Expert routing for efficient inference
